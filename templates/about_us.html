About us

Stackporfol.io was created out of a dev's need to showcase their portfolio easily, while making it visible to those looking to hire them.

By adding the technologies used on projects, dev's can show that they have proven experience with the tech stack that the company is looking for. Only when a technology is added to a project will it then be added to the dev's profile, which allows recruiters to trust they have the skills required for the job.
